This 3-book transformation trilogy was born from the seasons in my life when I finally stopped running, sat with myself and realized how much I had been carrying. My Philosophy

This is not about fixing you, for the simple reason that You are not broken. It is about partnership in knowing your self-worth, and understanding what a powerful being you are...

It is about having someone in your corner, wholly dedicated to the magnificent unfolding of your one, wild, and precious life.

These are not quick fixes or empty promises. It's a series of honest conversations and grounded, practical tools designed to be used, be it in the quiet of your morning, in the heat of a difficult moment or in the stillness of your night.

Think of it as a friend walking beside you, reminding you of your own strength when you forget, pointing you back to your own wisdom when you feel lost.

I RELEASE

There is a profound and often overlooked form of strength: the strength to let go.

Through simple, grounding affirmations paired with gentle reflections, this book guides you toward emotional freedom; the kind that comes from honesty, from slowing down, and from giving yourself permission to stop carrying what is no longer aligned with who you are becoming.

We are all carrying invisible weights; stories from yesterday, worries about tomorrow, expectations that never fit us, emotions that have overstayed their welcome. To say "I Release" is to conduct a gentle audit of your inner world and to consciously set down what is no longer yours to carry.

You are not discarding pieces of your soul; you are discarding the clutter that has gathered around it‚Ä¶ and there's no greater freedom, than letting go of that which no longer serves you.
